I'm a geospatial developer and serve as the Non-Academic Director on the AAG Cartography Specialty Group. My academic background focused heavily on cartography and remote sensing time-series analysis.

Lately, I've been enjoying collaborating with other geoviz folks to make compelling web maps and data visualizations in a variety of front-end JavaScript frameworks. I also occasionally find myself writing scientific algorithms to analyze complex Earth observation data.
