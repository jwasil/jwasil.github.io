A little blurb about yours truly

I'm a geospatial developer at Esri, with a strong academic background in raster and remote sensing time-series analysis.

Lately, I've been collaborating on making all kinds of compelling web mapping visualizations using SVG and Canvas techniques in a variety of front-end JavaScript frameworks.

Fun fact: When not doing this stuff, my mind is usually preoccupied with pizza and cats, of course.
